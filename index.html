How it works

Five steps from zero to
ops on your phone.

Any team. Any stack. Up and running in under 10 minutes — even if you don't have an MCP server yet.

1

Onboard your system

Run sarthi onboard. A guided intake captures your team, services, environments, and data platforms — then builds a complete system understanding for sárthí.

  • Captures Airflow, BigQuery, Trino, GCS, GitHub, Kafka, ServiceNow and many more
  • 11+ pre-built MCP servers and a growing library of sárthí skills included out of the box
  • Builds a deep system understanding — no manual configuration files
  • Can't find your system integration? sárthí scaffolds a local MCP server in under 10 minutes

2

Monitoring starts automatically

sárthí schedules a background agent that polls your stack every minute. Health dashboards, SLA tracking, and DAG run summaries are generated automatically — no manual setup.

  • Recurrent polling — always watching, even when you're away
  • Proactive health summaries and alerts delivered to your chat
  • Live session starts the moment someone reaches out — no setup needed

3

Patterns detected, issues solved

sárthí learns what "normal" looks like for your stack. Recurring failures are resolved without human intervention. Novel patterns are escalated with full diagnostic context.

  • Autonomous task clearing, pipeline re-triggering, service restarts
  • Simulate fixes safely in a dev environment before applying to production
  • Pattern library grows with every incident — smarter over time
  • Unknown patterns escalated with full diagnostic context

4

Chat-native notifications

sárthí pushes updates through a GitHub issue thread. Comment to ask, reply to act. Works on any BYOD device on your corporate network — no additional apps or dashboards needed.

  • GitHub issue as the chat channel — zero new tooling
  • Anyone on the team can ask and act from their phone
  • Full audit trail of every question, answer, and action

    && bash ~/sarthi/install.sh
Step 2 — onboard your team
$ sarthi onboard
