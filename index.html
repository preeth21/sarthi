Why sárthi

Your incident commander, on call 24/7.

sárthi unifies observability, runbooks, and remediation behind one conversational interface — so engineers spend minutes, not hours, restoring service.

Conversational ops

Diagnose

Root cause in seconds, not war rooms.

sárthi correlates logs, metrics, and traces across services to surface the actual cause — not just the symptom.

  • Cross-service log correlation
  • Anomaly detection on key SLIs
  • Auto-generated incident timelines
